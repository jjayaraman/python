### Notes
- Many methods (e.g., `sum`, `mean`, `var`) accept an `axis` parameter to operate along rows (`axis=1`) or columns (`axis=0`) in multi-dimensional arrays.
- Some are functions of the NumPy module (`np.`), while others are methods of the `ndarray` object (e.g., `arr.shape`).
- This isn’t exhaustive—NumPy has hundreds of methods—but these are the most widely used.
